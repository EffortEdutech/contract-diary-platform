## üéØ DECISION FRAMEWORK

### **Prioritization Criteria:**

**For Each Potential Feature/Expansion:**
1. **User Value** (1-10): How much does this help users?
2. **CIPAA Alignment** (1-10): Does this support compliance?
3. **Market Demand** (1-10): How many users want this?
4. **Technical Complexity** (1-10): How hard to build? (lower = easier)
5. **Cost** (1-10): What's the financial impact? (lower = cheaper)
6. **Time to Market** (1-10): How fast can we launch? (lower = faster)

**Total Score:** Sum of all criteria (max 60 points)

**Priority Levels:**
- **50-60 points:** CRITICAL - Do immediately
- **40-49 points:** HIGH - Plan for Phase 2
- **30-39 points:** MEDIUM - Consider for Phase 3
- **Below 30:** LOW - Future consideration
